The Road to Emmaus is a message of resurrection, of illuminating experiences, grace, and the institution of the Eucharist. “I have come to cast fire upon the earth; and how I wish it were already kindled!” Just as the Eucharist is the match that lights the fire - it’s up to all of us to kindle that flame and share it with everyone we encounter. Today it’s technology that allows us to fan the fire to a wider audience and share the knowledge of our beautiful Catholic faith in a way that opens the modern learner’s eyes and allows them to receive it in a way they prefer. The Road to Emmaus 2.0 is Online.
